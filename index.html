Academic Programs

The University of Michigan offers a wide range of highly regarded academic programs across various disciplines, including engineering, business, law, medicine, and the arts, consistently ranked among the top in the nation.

Academic Programs

Student Life

Student life at the University of Michigan is vibrant and diverse, with over 1,600 student organizations, a strong tradition of athletics, a thriving arts scene, and numerous opportunities for community involvement and leadership.

Student Life

Cost & Financial Aid

The University of Michigan's cost of attendance varies by residency status, but the university offers extensive financial aid through need-based grants, scholarships, work-study programs, and loans to help make education more affordable for students.

Cost & Financial Aid

Alumni Sucess

University of Michigan alumni are known for their remarkable success across various fields, benefiting from a strong network and a reputation for excellence that often leads to prominent careers in business, technology, healthcare, and public service.
