General Information


University of Michigan

The University of Michigan, located in Ann Arbor, Michigan, is one of the top public research universities in the U.S., known for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and leadership. Its mission emphasizes the creation, dissemination, and preservation of knowledge, as well as the development of leaders who will challenge the present and enrich the future. For more information, you can contact the university at (734) 764-1817 or visit their website at umich.edu.
